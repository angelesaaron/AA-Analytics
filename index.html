AA-Analytics

Sports analytics research portfolio featuring a suite of tools and models designed to assess player performance, evaluate betting markets, and analyze team strategy. This portfolio showcases predictive models built for data-driven decision-making in sports, with a focus on performance forecasting and market inefficiencies. It also includes a collection of research articles and blog posts that explore key trends in sports analytics. All projects and codebases are open source, leveraging major API services and statistical packages for data sourcing and analysis.

NFL

Big Game Gabe Davis Touchdown Model

Streamlit App

The Big Game Gabe Davis Touchdown Model is a Streamlit application built on a random forest machine learning model that predicts the likelihood of an NFL player scoring a touchdown in the upcoming week. The model processes a combination of comprehensive NFL box score statistics and situational data, including prior game performance, statistical team tendencies, and historical trends. It compares the model's predictions with the odds provided by popular sportsbooks, helping users identify potential value discrepancies. Additionally, the app provides a list of the week's best picks based on touchdown likelihood, along with the best value picks from each sportsbook provider. This tool offers sports enthusiasts and bettors a data-driven approach to evaluating player performances and finding profitable betting opportunities.

Playcall Profiler

Streamlit App

Playcall Profiler is a Streamlit application that aggregates the latest NFL play-by-play data for every game in the current season, providing users with detailed insights into playcalling tendencies. The app allows users to filter scenarios based on various factors such as down, distance, field position, and scoring margin, offering a comprehensive view of play distributions and scoring play probabilities. Fans can use the tool to gauge team strategies and understand the thought process behind playcalls, enhancing their viewing and gambling experience. By analyzing these scenarios, users can visualize trends in offensive and defensive strategies across different situations. Playcall Profiler empowers fans with a deeper understanding of game dynamics and the probability of specific outcomes based on playcall data.
